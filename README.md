# 🏠 Homents – Home Service Provider Platform

### Connecting Customers with Trusted Service Professionals

*Book. Manage. Get the Job Done.*



## 📖 Overview

**Homents** is a modern home service provider platform designed to connect customers with skilled professionals for various household services. The platform enables users to easily find, book, and manage service appointments while allowing workers to showcase their skills and receive service requests.

From plumbing and electrical repairs to cleaning and home maintenance, Homents provides a convenient and reliable solution for both customers and service providers.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Finding trustworthy home service professionals can be difficult and time-consuming. Customers often struggle with:

* Lack of verified service providers
* Difficulty comparing workers
* Unclear pricing and availability
* Poor communication during service requests
* Limited access to reviews and ratings

Homents addresses these challenges through a centralized digital platform.
